ATLANT Platform
What is ATLANT?

ATLANT is a global real estate blockchain platform.

ATLANT is addressing two of the most pertinent problems in real estate with its decentralized solution: Tokenized Ownership and P2P Rentals.

The platform is being developed as an open source framework which will provide both P2P rental network features (example: Airbnb) and tokenized ownership features, which will operate and trade on the Ethereum network, independent of the platform's contributors.

What are ATL tokens?

ATL tokens are essentially membership certificates in the ATLANT Platform, which give rights and privileges to their owners, as outlined in the ATLANT White Paper.

What are ATP tokens?

ATP is a pre-sale token, which will be automatically converted to ATL tokens after the ICO.

What is ICO's soft and hard cap?

ICO Soft cap is USD $1.5 million. Hard cap is 225,403 ETH.

What happens to tokens which are not sold during the ICO?

Any unsold tokens will be burned.

ADEX
What is ADEX?

ADEX stands for the ATLANT Decentralized Exchange. We have developed ADEX to allow for a safe and secure token trading. ADEX supports Bitcoin, Ether and many ERC20 tokens.

What are the advantages of ADEX?

ADEX connects token buyers and sellers by creating an organized market. It is powered by Ethereum smart contracts under the hood, allowing for a safe and secure way to transact between parties.

How to start?

Please click "SIGN UP" and proceed with your Email to set up an account in one minute. After verifying, you can fund your account with any token and start trading right away.

Where can I find trading and transaction fees?

Please refer to the Fees page. It contains both trading fees (fee per trade) and transaction fees (fee per token deposit or withdrawal).

How to quickly swap tokens?

If you intend to change tokens quickly, you are welcome to use the Token Swap form.

This form allows you to exchange tokens at the market price without any special knowledge. For instance, you can change BTC for ATL in a single click after specifying amount of bitcoins to sell and vice versa.

Security
Is it secure to trade at ADEX?

ADEX follows best security practices and takes all necessary precautions in its operations. Smart contract code is audited and well-tested.

We facilitate user account security by providing 2-factor authentication (2FA) via SMS and Telegram. 2FA is an additional layer of security above password and an effective way to protect your account. We recommend turning on 2FA option to increase your account security.

How to turn on 2FA?

You can turn on 2-factor authentication by clicking "ACTIVATE" at 2FA section on the Settings page
